Audience Demographics

The following primary audiences are women and online hobbists.

Demographic categories and statistics
Women According to Stadista there are 164.05m females in U.S. and from those women 68.08 are married. According to the UNITED STATES DEPARTMENT OF LABOR a high percentage of women work as full-time elementary and middle school teachers, are between 35-44 years old and are white.
Online Hobbyists The Pew Research Center says that Hobbyists that use the internet are 18-29 years old, have some college or more than a college degree, their income is greater or equal to $75,000, are white, and most are males, but the gender gap is not big.
